<h1 class="q-article-title q-skip-small">Content Policy</h1>

All of the content I produce is (and will always be) available for free---shared under a Creative Commons or equivalent licence. You're also free to share, remix, and build upon it as you wish, as long as you follow some simple rules.

  - **No commercial use**. You're not allowed to sell the content directly and you're not allowed to make a profit out of content you remix or build upon. If you're an individual (i.e.: not a company), then it's fine to make a profit out of content you remix or build upon.

  - **Give attribution**. Especially if you're sharing the content directly, give credits where they're due.

  - **Don't be offensive**. Don't use any content I produce in contexts that are racist, sexist, homophobic, transphobic, ableist, or that would otherwise be in violation of my [code of conduct](/coc/).
